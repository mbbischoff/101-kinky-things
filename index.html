Let’s get acquainted

Kate Sloan is a journalist, author, blogger, podcaster, and educator with over a decade of sex writing under her belt (so to speak). Her words on sex, kink, and relationships have appeared in publications such as Cosmopolitan, GQ, MEL, Glamour, Teen Vogue, Playboy, and Insider. She is the cohost of the acclaimed podcasts The Dildorks and Question Box, and her award-winning sex blog GirlyJuice.net is a go-to resource for many. 101 Kinky Things is her first book. Her second book, 200 Words to Help You Talk About Gender & Sexuality, was released in May 2022.
